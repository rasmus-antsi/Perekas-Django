Our Story

Rooted in family life, designed for joyful productivity

FamilyHub was founded by parents who wanted to transform chore charts into moments of connection. Today, we're powering households around the globe.

Mission

To empower households with tools that combine clarity, celebration, and collaboration—so families spend less time coordinating and more time thriving together.

Vision

A world where every family, regardless of shape or size, enjoys smooth routines, shared ownership, and joyful celebrations of progress.

Team

We're designers, parents, educators, and dreamers. Together, we listen closely to the households using FamilyHub and build alongside them.

Milestones

From kitchen table idea to global platform

  1. 2019

    We prototype the first FamilyHub to help our own kids stay on top of chores.

  2. 2021

    Public beta launches, bringing in the first 500 families who shape today’s experience.

  3. 2023

    Rewards marketplace and shared shopping list go live, helping families celebrate wins.

  4. 2024

    FamilyHub powers 12k+ households and introduces automations that keep routines flowing on autopilot.

What guides us

Values baked into every pixel

Empathy first

We build with families—not just for them. Every decision starts with listening.

Celebrate progress

Always make room for delight. Every action should feel rewarding, not draining.

Simple is powerful

Clarity, contrast, and motion guide our design. Busy lives deserve effortless tools.
