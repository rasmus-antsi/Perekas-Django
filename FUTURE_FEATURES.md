# Future Features - Family Management SaaS

## üéØ High-Value Features (Most Requested by Families)

### 1. **Recurring Tasks & Chores**
**Why it helps:** Families have many repetitive tasks (take out trash, feed pets, weekly chores)
- Daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly recurrence options
- Auto-create tasks on schedule
- "Skip this occurrence" option
- Different recurrence patterns (every Monday, first of month, etc.)
- **Impact:** Saves parents hours of manual task creation

### 2. **Family Calendar & Events**
**Why it helps:** Coordinate schedules, appointments, and family activities
- Shared family calendar view
- Events (doctor appointments, school events, birthdays)
- Task due dates visible on calendar
- Color-coding by family member
- Calendar sync with Google Calendar/iCal
- **Impact:** Reduces scheduling conflicts and missed appointments

### 3. **Task Categories & Tags**
**Why it helps:** Organize tasks by type (chores, homework, responsibilities)
- Categories: Kitchen, Bathroom, Yard, Homework, Pet Care, etc.
- Filter tasks by category
- Category-based point multipliers
- Category statistics (which areas need more help)
- **Impact:** Better organization and helps identify patterns

### 4. **Smart Notifications & Reminders**
**Why it helps:** Keep everyone on track without constant nagging
- Push notifications for task assignments
- Reminders before due dates (1 day, 3 days, 1 week)
- Email digests (daily/weekly summaries)
- In-app notifications for task completions/approvals
- Customizable notification preferences per user
- **Impact:** Improves task completion rates significantly

### 5. **Activity Feed & Family Timeline**
**Why it helps:** See what everyone's been up to, celebrate achievements
- Real-time activity feed (who completed what, when)
- Achievement highlights (milestones, streaks)
- Family member highlights
- Photo sharing for completed tasks
- Weekly/monthly family summaries
- **Impact:** Increases engagement and motivation
